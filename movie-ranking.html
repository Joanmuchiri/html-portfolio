Joan's Best Movies

The Best Movies according to Joan

My top 3 movies of all times

Game of Thrones

Complex character and storyline. Tyrion made the show much interesting with his humour.

How To Get Away With Murder

The flash backs and fast forwards made the series so catchy. Annalise Keating was a well executed character.

Prison Break

Who doesn't want to know what it will take for someone to break out of prison.
